Objective

Design and implement a proof-of-concept (POC) system where an AI Agent monitors an email inbox, reads incoming messages, categorizes them, and produces a daily summary email. The summary should clearly distinguish between:
	1.	Items Requiring Attention – Messages that need action or response.
	2.	Items to Review Later – Messages that are informational and may be useful at a later stage.
	3.	Items of No Immediate Relevance – Messages that can be ignored (e.g., spam, promotions).

⸻

Challenge Requirements

1. Email Access
	•	Connect securely to a mailbox (Gmail, Outlook, or a test IMAP inbox).
	•	Retrieve unread emails for processing.

2. AI Summarization Agent
	•	Use a secure LLM approach. Options include:
	•	A local/self-hosted open-source LLM.
	•	An API-based LLM with safeguards (e.g., anonymizing/redacting sensitive content before sending).
	•	Summarize each email in a concise, human-readable form.
	•	Categorize each email into one of the three defined categories.
	•	Generate a single consolidated daily summary.

3. Output / Reporting
	•	The AI Agent should send the summary as an email to the same mailbox (or a designated reporting email).
	•	The summary must be presented in a professional, business-friendly format as follows:

⸻

Daily Email Digest

Items Requiring Attention
	•	[Sender, Subject] – Concise summary highlighting the key issue or action needed.
	•	[Sender, Subject] – Concise summary highlighting the key issue or action needed.

Items to Review Later
	•	[Sender, Subject] – Summary of content that is informative but not urgent.
	•	[Sender, Subject] – Summary of content that can be retained for future reference.

Items of No Immediate Relevance
	•	[Sender, Subject] – Brief note on why the email can be disregarded (e.g., promotional content, spam).

⸻

Security & Data Safety
	•	Ensure confidentiality of email content at all times.
	•	Avoid hardcoding credentials; use environment variables or secure credential storage.
	•	If using an external LLM API, implement a step to mask or redact personal identifiers before sending text for processing.

⸻

Expectations

We do not expect a production-ready solution. Instead, we are evaluating your ability to:
	•	Design a clear and logical system architecture.
	•	Demonstrate awareness of data privacy and security.
	•	Write clean, modular code (Python preferred).
	•	Document your thought process and decisions.
